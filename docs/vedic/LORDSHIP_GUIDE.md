# Lordship Guide

## Parashari Lordship Principles

The functional nature of a planet depends on which houses it owns FOR THE
SPECIFIC LAGNA — not its natural beneficence. This is the foundational
principle of Parashari Jyotish.

### Key Rules (from BPHS)

1. **Kendra lords (1, 4, 7, 10):** Natural benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury,
   Moon) lose beneficence when owning kendras — this is **Kendradhipati Dosha**.
   Natural malefics (Saturn, Mars) gain beneficence.

2. **Trikona lords (1, 5, 9):** Always functionally benefic regardless of
   natural status. Trikonas are the most auspicious houses.

3. **Dusthana lords (3, 6, 8, 12):** Functionally malefic. Strengthening these
   lords amplifies their negative house significations.

4. **Yogakaraka:** A single planet owning both a kendra AND a trikona becomes
   the most auspicious planet for that lagna. Examples: Mars for Karka (5th+10th),
   Saturn for Tula (4th+5th).

5. **Maraka planets:** Lords of 2nd and 7th houses are maraka (death-inflicting).
   During their dashas, they can bring health crises.

6. **Lagna lord:** Always benefic — owns both a kendra (1st) and trikona (1st).

## 12-Lagna Summary

### Mesha (Aries)
- **Lagnesh:** Mars (1st+8th) — benefic, 8th secondary
- **Yogakaraka:** Sun (5th) — pure trikona
- **Maraka:** Venus (2nd+7th) — double maraka
- **Malefics:** Mercury (3rd+6th), Saturn (10th+11th)

### Vrishabha (Taurus)
- **Lagnesh:** Venus (1st+6th) — benefic, 6th secondary
- **Yogakaraka:** Saturn (9th+10th) — classic kendra+trikona
- **Maraka:** Mars (7th+12th), Mercury (2nd+5th, mild)
- **Malefics:** Jupiter (8th+11th), Moon (3rd)

### Mithuna (Gemini)
- **Lagnesh:** Mercury (1st+4th) — anchor planet
- **Yogakaraka:** Venus (5th+12th) — strongest benefic
- **Maraka:** Moon (2nd), Jupiter (7th+10th, Kendradhipati dosha)
- **Malefics:** Mars (6th+11th), Jupiter (7th+10th), Sun (3rd)

### Karka (Cancer)
- **Lagnesh:** Moon (1st) — single lordship, always protect
- **Yogakaraka:** Mars (5th+10th) — textbook yogakaraka
- **Maraka:** Saturn (7th+8th), Sun (2nd)
- **Malefics:** Venus (4th+11th), Mercury (3rd+12th)

### Simha (Leo)
- **Lagnesh:** Sun (1st) — single lordship
- **Yogakaraka:** Mars (4th+9th) — kendra+trikona
- **Maraka:** Saturn (6th+7th), Mercury (2nd+11th)
- **Malefics:** Venus (3rd+10th), Saturn (6th+7th)

### Kanya (Virgo)
- **Lagnesh:** Mercury (1st+10th) — double kendra
- **Yogakaraka:** Venus (2nd+9th) — trikona dominant
- **Maraka:** Jupiter (4th+7th), Mars (3rd+8th)
- **Malefics:** Mars (3rd+8th), Jupiter (7th, Kendradhipati)

### Tula (Libra)
- **Lagnesh:** Venus (1st+8th) — benefic, 8th secondary
- **Yogakaraka:** Saturn (4th+5th) — classic yogakaraka
- **Maraka:** Mars (2nd+7th) — double maraka
- **Malefics:** Jupiter (3rd+6th), Sun (11th)

### Vrischika (Scorpio)
- **Lagnesh:** Mars (1st+6th) — benefic, 6th secondary
- **Yogakaraka:** Jupiter (2nd+5th) — trikona+dhana
- **Maraka:** Venus (7th+12th), Jupiter (2nd)
- **Malefics:** Mercury (8th+11th), Venus (7th+12th)

### Dhanu (Sagittarius)
- **Lagnesh:** Jupiter (1st+4th) — double kendra
- **Yogakaraka:** Mars (5th+12th) — trikona dominant
- **Maraka:** Saturn (2nd+3rd), Mercury (7th+10th)
- **Malefics:** Venus (6th+11th), Saturn (2nd+3rd)

### Makara (Capricorn)
- **Lagnesh:** Saturn (1st+2nd) — also maraka house
- **Yogakaraka:** Venus (5th+10th) — classic yogakaraka
- **Maraka:** Moon (7th), Saturn (2nd, self-maraka)
- **Malefics:** Mars (4th+11th), Jupiter (3rd+12th)

### Kumbha (Aquarius)
- **Lagnesh:** Saturn (1st+12th) — benefic, 12th secondary
- **Yogakaraka:** Venus (4th+9th) — kendra+trikona
- **Maraka:** Sun (7th), Mercury (2nd+5th, mild)
- **Malefics:** Jupiter (2nd+11th), Mars (3rd+10th)

### Meena (Pisces)
- **Lagnesh:** Jupiter (1st+10th) — double kendra
- **Yogakaraka:** Mars (2nd+9th) — trikona+dhana
- **Maraka:** Mercury (4th+7th), Mars (2nd)
- **Malefics:** Saturn (11th+12th), Venus (3rd+8th)
